Studio Etiquette

Please remove outdoor shoes before entering the studio.

Please arrive 5-10 minutes before the class starts. Due to health and safety reasons, students will not be permitted to participate in any class if they are more than 10 minutes late.

DO NOT use any lotions, oils, creams or moisturisers on the day of your class. Greasy poles and floors are dangerous!

No food or beverages are to be consumed within the studio. Bottled water allowed only

Always advise the instructor of any injuries or medical issues before the start of class.

Remove all jewellery that may scratch the poles, i.e rings, bangles, bracelets e.t.c.

General Policies

All new students must sign a waiver prior to participating in any class, pole party or workshop.

To secure a spot for any of the classes, you MUST register online beforehand. If you drop in without registering, you will only be given a place if there is space available.

We DO NOT offer refunds, so please check your booking before making a purchase. All prices are subject to change without notification.

Bookings must be made in advance online. We cannot reserve a place or guarantee there is room for you in the class without a payment online beforehand. It is best to book in advance or you might not get a spot.

Cancellations

If you need to cancel a class, you must email soulpoleworld@gmail.com 12 hours before the scheduled class time. Once an email has been received before the 12 hours, a member of the Soul Pole team will forward you an email to inform you that you can use your credit for another class. Any time after the 12 hours will be considered as a late cancel and you will not receive a refund or credit for the class missed.



Class Policies

If you are more than 10 minutes late to a class, we reserve the right to refuse your attendance to class as you may have missed the crucial warm up. For health and safety reasons, no student is allowed to attend class without a proper warm up. You should be dressed and ready to start at the scheduled class time. We suggest that you arrive 5-10 minutes before your scheduled class. We ask that you respect both instructors and students by showing up on time.


Taking pictures and filming is allowed during our classes, however you MUST respect the privacy of other students. If you want to take pictures or film yourself, you are welcome to do so with the permission of the instructor at designated times and these times will be indicated during your class. Please do not take any photographs or film other students without their permission as you will be asked to leave the class if you do so. Photographs of instructors or students are not to be uploaded onto any social media websites without written consent or authorisation.


Practice classes are meant for practicing moves you already know. Do not attempt to perform new moves during a practice class. You may not use practice classes to teach a friend new pole moves. You may, however, review what you had learned in class with other students. Remember an instructor is always there if you need spotting and there are also mats provided for your safety.


Please respect your instructor and fellow students and leave your phone conversations outside the studio while in class. This also goes to text messaging as well. We do not tolerate bullying or disrespecting of other students and their personal space. Such behaviour will result in removal from the studio and you will be banned from taking any classes at Soul Pole.


Party Policies

Pole Party dates must be secured with full payment before the party is booked. Please email soulpoleworld@gmail.com with your prefered party date and time, brief information i.e birthday, hen party e.t.c and any special requirements and a member of the team will get right back to you.


Reservations﻿

We offer parties between 4pm-8pm Saturdays. You can book up-to several weeks or even months in advance. Reservations are required and are taken on a first-come, first-serve basis. We highly recommend booking your preferred date and time as soon as possible.


Rescheduling/Reservation Changes﻿

Parties can be rescheduled once if given a 10 day notice (subject to availability). If you would like to change your party package or start time, please give us a call or send an email to soulpoleworld@gmail.com at least 10 days before your party and we will try our best to accommodate the change. Changes cannot be made less than 10 days before the party because our resources have already been allocated.


Cancellations

Here at Soul Pole, we understand that sometimes life gets in the way. Because of this, we allow party bookings to be cancelled up to 10 days before the original party date. Our cancellation fee is 50% of the original party package booked.


Participation

Every person attending your party is considered a participant and counted into the pricing as such, even if he/she does not participate or arrives late/leaves early. If you are pregnant or have an injury, please check with your doctor first before participating. All attendees must also be at least 18 years old.
