About

About Us
Soul Pole pride ourselves on offering a professional pole fitness training that is flexible enough to fit around your schedule to help you achieve whatever your fitness goal may be. We aim to provide a professional and friendly training environment for women and men to pole, train and laugh together, encouraging each other to develop their pole skills, build strength, increase confidence and work towards a newer stronger and more powerful you.

The alternative style of Pole allows inhibitions to be stripped and replaced by a new found sense of fun and energy. It's perfect for all abilities, so it doesn't matter if you've never had a go before - you'll be provided with an experienced instructor who will show you all the best techniques! We aim to create a safe, welcoming, supportive and encouraging environment for you to discover just what pole fitness can do for you. Our classes are intimate and small to provide you with the quality, comfort and the reassurance you need to become a part of our friendly family atmosphere. All our instructors are certified and have a range of abilities that will have you expressing and celebrating your own individuality and dance style in no time.

What Is Pole Fitness?

Pole fitness is a whole body workout that combines strength, flexibility and cardio exercises with dance moves unique to your own dance style. It is incredibly exhilarating, hard work, artistic and highly addictive. With each move centred on a vertical pole, you will not only develop core & muscular strength, but will be able to boost your confidence and self-esteem, whilst learning some exceptionally impressive moves. Each pole fitness class at Soul Pole is carefully structured to teach you pole fitness safely and effectively. We cater for levels of abilities. Whether you are a newbie/beginner or an experienced pole dancer, we will have the right class for you.

Benefits of Pole Fitness

  • Builds confidence
  • Burns calories quickly = weight loss
  • Decreases stress & anxiety
  • Improves flexibility
  • Aids a better night sleep
  • Meet new people, make friends and become a part of the ever growing pole community
