Description

As certified instructors, we will patiently help you to achieve your pole fitness goals in a private setting. This way lessons are tailored to your individual needs and you will have the dedicated time you need to practice and perfect the moves you want to. Private lessons are a great way to build your confidence if you prefer not to join in a class setting. Private lessons can be offered as part of group (max 3 people) or on an individual basis.

Private lessons can be arranged in the studio or within the comfort of your own home. To book a private lesson, please contact us below or email soulpoleworld@gmail.com and state your preference and we will get right back to you.
