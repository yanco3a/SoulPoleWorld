Description
The Beginner's Pole course will take your skills from Try Pole to the next level. You will now learn to master those all important spins, tricks and climbs to get you to work up a sweat, move with grace and elegance and prepare you for what's to come at the Intermediate level. Your instructor will break down every step in great detail so that you can start increasing your body awareness and learn how and when to properly engage certain muscles. You will also learn to how to move smoothly around the pole and follow simple choreography. The techniques learnt in this class will build your strength and prepare you for those inversions. Yes, we're going upside down!

* This class is offered as part of a 4-week course or single drop in classes to work around your busy lifestyle.
What to wear
Please wear shorts, t-shirt or vest top, no jewellery and absolutely NO oils, lotions or moisturiser for at least 12 hours before the class.
Prerequisite
Must have taken a Try Pole class.
